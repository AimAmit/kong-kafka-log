Kong Kafka Log Plugin
Copyright 2020 Optum


Project Description:
====================

The Kong Kafka Log is a small, lua-based, OpenResty Nginx module which send logs to Kafka based on Kong’s proxy requests. The purpose of this Kafka plugin is to maintain meta-data logs of all transactions.
